## 2. Why We'll Win

- **Every reason gets a "so what do I do" line.** All four competitors stop at *what* and *why* — none tell the reader how to act on the reason. Our existing page was flagged for exactly this gap. We add a one-line action step to each of the 12 reasons.
- **We cut the duplication that hurts the current page.** The incumbent repeats reasons (track progress ×2, sell business ×2). We ship 12 genuinely distinct reasons, each earning its place.
- **We frame the inverse — what skipping a plan actually costs.** No competitor covers the cost of *not* planning. This is a unique section backed by failure-rate data.
- **Real data, properly sourced.** Competitors cite "30% faster growth" and "16% more likely" with vague attribution. We tie each stat to its primary source (HBR, Babson/effectuation research, SBA, CB Insights).
- **Stage-based nuance.** SBA and SBDC hint that some businesses can plan lean; none give a clear "who needs the full plan vs. who can keep it one page" answer. We do.
