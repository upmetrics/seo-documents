## 4. SERP & INTENT ANALYSIS

**What Google is rewarding:**
- **Dominant intent:** Informational — comparison/explanation. Users want to know which document to make and when.
- **Content types ranking:** 5/10 are dedicated comparison blog posts; 3/10 are discussion threads (Reddit ×2, Quora ×1); 1/10 is our own help-doc; 1/10 is a YouTube video (in AI Overview sitelinks).
- **What this means:** Google strongly favors comprehensive comparison articles with explicit "when to use each" framing. The presence of 3 discussion threads tells us users want personal-experience answers — winning content should address sequencing questions (which comes first, do I need both).

**Intent match/mismatch:**
- **Current page type:** Blog comparison article ✅ correct format
