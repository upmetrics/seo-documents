## REVISION PRIORITY MATRIX

### P0 — Blockers — Fix Before Publishing

**WRONG (factual errors — fix these first):**
1. **50/100/500 rule is unverified** — FAQ section — The well-known "50/100/500 rule" defines when a startup is no longer a startup (Alex Wilhelm, TechCrunch), not validation benchmarks. Either cite a source for this validation version or reframe without calling it a named "rule."
2. **Pronoun error: "my idea"** — Tier-1 problem section — "don't lead with my idea" should be "don't lead with your idea." Article uses second person throughout.
3. **Garbled sentence in MVP section** — "test one if someone pays" should be "test if someone pays." Stray word "one" breaks meaning.

**MISSING (critical absent content — fill these second):**
4. **Go/no-go section is 88 words** — "How to know when you've validated enough" — This is the brief's #1 differentiator (UNIQUE CONTENT GAP). Expand to 300-400 words with concrete thresholds: 20-30 interview saturation, 2%+ landing page CVR, 3-5 independent paying customers, repeat usage, unprompted referrals.
5. **Content cuts off mid-thought** — Same section — "Here's how I read them:" is followed by nothing. The promised list/table of signals never appears.
6. **All 6 must-include keywords missing** — Entire article body — "how to validate your business idea," "how to test your business idea," "how to validate a startup idea," "how to evaluate a business idea," "validate startup idea," "business idea validation" — none appear in the published content.
7. **No failed-validation guidance** — "What comes after" section — Article never addresses what to do when validation fails. Add pivot/abandon/reframe guidance before the business plan transition.

**WEAK (content exists but too shallow — deepen these third):**
8. **Prototype/demo paragraph unclear** — "When you only need to demonstrate to someone how to work" is missing an object. Entire P45 needs rewrite for clarity.
9. **Scope-narrowing advice is vague** — P46 says "narrow the scope" but never explains how. Add: "Pick the ONE assumption that, if wrong, kills the idea."
10. **FAQ voice drift** — FAQs use formal third-person ("one should have approximately") vs. article's conversational first-person. Rewrite to match.
11. **P45 awkward phrasing** — "not to merely ask to give feedback" should be "don't just ask for feedback."
12. **P46 passive/hedging** — "I would highly suggest having the scope narrowed" should be "Narrow your scope."
13. **Section balance** — "7 Ways" is 1,534 words (53%) while go/no-go is 88 words (2.4%). Reallocate.
14. **Capterra link wrong domain** — Points to capterra.in (India) instead of capterra.com (US).
15. **4 empty anchor hyperlinks** — Invisible links at Google Trends, market research, industry analysis, and Upmetrics solutions page. Remove or merge.
