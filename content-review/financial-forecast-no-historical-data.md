## REVISION PRIORITY MATRIX

### P0 -- Blockers -- Fix Before Publishing

**WRONG (factual errors, contradictions -- fix these first):**
1. **Table number inconsistency** -- Expense table (Step 4), cash flow table (Step 5), and scenario table all use different expense figures for the same SaaS example. Month 1: $11,660 vs $11,900. Month 7-12: $11,220 vs $8,100. Annual: $114,730 vs $98,000. Reconcile ALL tables.
2. **SBA forecast timeline** -- Article says "3 years is the most widely accepted standard" and dismisses 5-year forecasts. SBA lenders often require 3-5 years. Could lead readers to submit insufficient projections.
3. **Buffer contradiction** -- Step 4 says 10-15% buffer, Step 5 says 15-20%. Pick one consistent approach.
4. **"Three things" contradiction** -- Second section says "you need three things" but Step 2 covers four sources (missing bottom-up modeling).
5. **Cash flow timeline claim** -- "12 to 18 months of monthly projections" understates SBA requirements (typically first 12-24 months as part of 3-5 year forecast).
6. **Income statement formula** -- Lists taxes/interest as final step but example table skips them entirely.
7. **Grammar errors** -- "the only nature" (should be "the very nature"), "how to do:" (missing "it").

**MISSING (critical absent content -- fill these second):**
8. **Conclusion section** -- Brief requires this (MUST HAVE, ~150 words). Article ends abruptly after FAQs.
9. **Common Mistakes section** -- Brief requires this (SHOULD HAVE, ~200 words). Differentiation opportunity.
10. **All 5 must-include keywords** -- None appear in article body text (only in H1 and FAQ headings). Critical SEO gap.
11. **Primary keyword in first 100 words** -- Missing. Must appear naturally in the opening paragraph.
12. **Internal links** -- No links to 4 highly relevant Bizplanr blog posts (financial-forecasting-models, financial-projection-mistakes, cash-flow-vs-profit, how-to-create-a-cash-flow-forecast).
13. **SCORE.org external link** -- Brief requires it; completely missing.

**WEAK (content exists but too shallow -- deepen these third):**
14. **"Can you actually create..." section** -- Only 83 words, repeats the intro. Brief called for "Why You Need a Forecast Before Your First Sale" (~200 words) with business reasons.
15. **Balance sheet** -- No worked example table, unlike every other step. Add a simple 3-line example.
16. **Sales forecast churn** -- Table has implicit churn (varying 0-5/month) with no explanation. Add a "Churned" column and explain the rate.
17. **Condescending tone** -- "Obviously yes" in balance sheet section is dismissive to beginner audience.
18. **"w/o" abbreviation** -- In H2 heading; spell out "without."
