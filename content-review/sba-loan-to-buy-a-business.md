## REVISION PRIORITY MATRIX

### P0 — Blockers — Fix Before Publishing

**WRONG (factual errors, contradictions — fix these first):**
1. **SBA 504 CDC rate is incorrect** — "SBA 7(a) vs. SBA 504" section — Article says ~6.7%–7.5%, actual Jan 2026 rates are ~5.67%–5.91%. Update paragraph AND comparison table.
2. **Seller note interest claim is misleading** — "Deal structure" section — "Seller isn't earning interest" is wrong. Interest accrues during standby; it's just not paid until the SBA loan is satisfied. Clarify.
3. **Non-PLP delay inconsistency** — "5 Mistakes" section — Claims "3-6 weeks" but Step 5 and Timeline sections both say "1-3 weeks." Align to "1-3 additional weeks."
4. **Intro contradicts cited statistic** — Introduction — Says "tens of thousands" but later cites "9,500–10,000." Change to "thousands."
5. **Image 1 (Approval Checklist) has 3 data conflicts** — After "US citizenship" section — Financials: says 2 Years (should be 3). Experience: says 2+ Years (not in text). Owner-Operator: says 51%+ (not in text). Update infographic.
6. **Image 2 (Timeline) has 4 data conflicts** — After Step 7 — Underwriting: says 1 Week (should be 2-4). Funding: says 1 Week (should be 1 day). Step names differ from article. Redesign.
7. **SBA Form 413 links to third-party site (2 locations)** — Step 4 + FAQ — Links to 1stfinancialassociates.com instead of official sba.gov/document/sba-form-413-personal-financial-statement. Update both.

**MISSING (critical absent content):**
8. **Personal guarantee requirement** — "Qualify" section — No mention that anyone with 20%+ ownership must personally guarantee the SBA loan. This is a critical qualification factor.
9. **Zero inline internal links in article body** — Throughout — Brief recommends 7 internal links. Article body has 0. Add at minimum: /blog/what-is-an-sba-loan, /blog/sba-loan-requirements, /blog/best-sba-lenders.
10. **3 empty anchor hyperlinks** — Step 4 + Conclusion — Hidden empty hyperlinks to lendermatch.sba.gov, /blog/sba-loan-business-plan, /templates. Remove orphaned tags.

**LANGUAGE (tone/brand voice):**
11. **Introduction filler and tone** — Intro paragraphs — "You got it. SBA loans." / "even for you!" / "shall we?" doesn't match the expert advisor voice. Rewrite first 3-4 paragraphs.
12. **"Bringing in the bag" + emoji** — "Why consider buying" section — Slang with emoji not brand voice. Replace with "already generating revenue."
13. **"Even for you!" is condescending** — Introduction — Remove.
