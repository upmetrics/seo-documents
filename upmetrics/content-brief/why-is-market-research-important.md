## 2. Why We'll Win

- **Business planning angle no competitor covers** — We connect market research directly to business plan creation, funding pitches, and financial forecasting. Every competitor talks about "why it matters" generically; none show how market research feeds into a business plan that gets funded.
- **Actionable how-to section with free tools** — Most competitors stop at "reasons why." We include a step-by-step guide with specific free tools and resources (SBA.gov data, Census Bureau, Google Trends) that readers can use immediately.
- **Common mistakes section** — No competitor covers what to avoid. We add a unique "mistakes to avoid" section that prevents readers from wasting time on bad research practices.
- **Entrepreneur-focused perspective** — Competitors target enterprise/agency audiences (Kantar, Cint, Hanover are B2B research firms). We speak directly to startup founders and small business owners planning their first venture.
