Item 15 separately lists your obligations as the franchisee."

**MISSING (fill these second):**

Add a two-column sample table (line items + example figures for a generic franchise unit) or delete the heading. An empty section heading undermines credibility more than no section at all.
6. **Operations plan duplicate sentence** — Supply Chain subsection — "Most franchisors provide staffing benchmarks based on store size or projected volume" is copied word-for-word from the Staffing subsection above and pasted into Supply Chain where it doesn't belong. Delete it from Supply Chain and write content specific to vendors and approved supplier lists.
7. **Empty hyperlink in Conclusion** — Conclusion — A hyperlink to the franchise business plan template exists with no anchor text. Invisible to readers and search engines. Delete the empty hyperlink (the template is already linked correctly two sentences later).
