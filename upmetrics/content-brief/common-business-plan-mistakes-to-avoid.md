## 2. Why We'll Win

- **LivePlan gives generic advice; we give specifics with real numbers.** LivePlan says "have realistic projections" — we'll show actual examples: a hockey-stick forecast vs. a credible one, with the specific assumptions investors flag.
- **Investor/lender POV that LivePlan completely skips.** LivePlan tells you what's wrong with your plan from the writer's perspective. We tell you what an SBA loan officer or angel investor actually rejects in the first 5 minutes — and what red flag they're pattern-matching to.
- **Each mistake includes a "fix" with a concrete before/after example.** LivePlan describes mistakes but rarely shows what "fixed" looks like. We pair every mistake with a 2-3 sentence example of the wrong way and a corrected version.
- **Grouped by impact (funding-killing vs execution-killing) — not a flat list.** LivePlan dumps 17 mistakes in one undifferentiated list. We group ours by what they cost you: mistakes that get your plan rejected by lenders/investors, vs. mistakes that hurt how you run the business after launch.
