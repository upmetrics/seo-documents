### P0 -- Blockers -- Fix Before Publishing

**WRONG (factual errors -- fix these first):**
1. **Section 179 limit outdated** -- FAQ section -- The $1,220,000 limit is the 2024 figure. Update to $2,560,000 (2026 limit). This FAQ is a prime featured snippet candidate.
2. **Break-even rounding error** -- Cash Flow Impact section -- Math shows 7.84 months, not 7. Change to "around month 8."
3. **ROI defined incorrectly** -- Mistakes section (Mistake 3) -- What you describe (time to recoup investment) is payback period, not ROI. ROI is a percentage. Fix the definition or rename to "payback period."
4. **Bonus depreciation understated** -- FAQ section -- 100% bonus depreciation was reinstated for 2025-2026. Update the vague "may also apply" to state this explicitly.

**MISSING (critical gaps -- fill these second):**
5. **All CapEx keywords missing** -- "Why plan?" section -- Must-include keywords "capital expenditure planning" and "capex budget" (400/mo combined) appear zero times. Add a bridge sentence introducing these terms.

7. **Zero outbound links in body** -- FAQ + Financing sections -- Brief requires IRS Section 179, SBA Programs, Fed Credit Survey links. Add all three.
8. **No internal links in financing section** -- Cash vs. Loan vs. Lease section -- Add link to SBA loan guide (https://upmetrics.co/blog/what-is-an-sba-loan).

**WEAK (content exists but too shallow -- deepen third):**
9. **Loan payments without interest rates** -- Cash vs. Loan vs. Lease section -- All four payment figures ($480, $510, $390, $280/mo) lack stated interest rates and terms. Readers cannot verify or adjust.
10. **SBA loan payment without rate/term** -- Same section -- $390/mo and $27,300 total need rate and term stated.
