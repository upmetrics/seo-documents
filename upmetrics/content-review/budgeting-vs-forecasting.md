## P0 BLOCKERS

**WRONG** (factual/arithmetic errors — fix first):

1. **Q1 example math contradiction** — Examples of budgeting vs. forecasting — "$250K of revenue, which is double that of the budget, which was pegged at $300K" contradicts the variance table four paragraphs down. Rewrite as "$250K, roughly 17% short of the $300K budget."
2. **Q1 variance table doesn't reconcile** — Examples of budgeting vs. forecasting — Plan OI $51K minus revenue drop ($50K) plus shown COGS savings ($12K) = $13K actual, not the $22K shown. Add missing expense-line variances or recalculate.
3. **Lead stat overstates CB Insights** — Introduction — CB Insights' actual figure is 70% ("ran out of capital") on the 431-company 2024 dataset; "almost three-quarters" reads as ~75%. Tighten to "70%" or "seven in ten of failed startups."
4. **FAQ 5-steps list missing Step 5** — Frequently asked questions — H3 promises "5 steps" but the bullet list has only 4. Add the 5th: "Using the numbers to make decisions about hiring, spending, and reforecasting."
5. **image2.png placement + decorative-only charts** — End of doc — A "Budgeting vs Forecasting" hero infographic sits inside the FAQ '5 steps of budgeting' answer. Move to article top or under "Budgeting vs forecasting at a glance," and either re-render the inner charts with article numbers or label as illustrative.
6. **5 stray empty hyperlinks** — Multiple sections — Zero-width links sit before each cited stat (AFP 96%, forecasting methods, Salesforce, FP&A Trends, AFP research). Open the doc and delete the empty hyperlink immediately preceding each visible citation.

**MISSING** (critical absent content — fill second):

1. **Two must-include keywords absent** — Comparison table caption + Introduction — "budget vs forecast" (KD position 71.8) and "difference between budget and forecast" (KD position 67.5) never appear in the article body. Rename the table caption to "Budget vs forecast at a glance" and add an intro sentence using "difference between budget and forecast."
