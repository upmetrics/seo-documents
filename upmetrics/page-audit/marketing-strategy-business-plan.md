### Top 3 Problems

1. **The page has impressions but earns zero clicks.** GSC shows 241 ranking queries, ~2,000+ total impressions over 6 months, **0 clicks**. Every meaningful query ranks position 55-90+. The page is invisible.
2. **The page targets the wrong title.** Title tag = "Marketing Strategy for Your Business" but the H1 + body + URL = "for Business Plan." Google can't reconcile the two — and the cluster the page actually accumulates impressions for ("marketing business plan," "marketing plan for startup," "marketing strategy business plan") is the *business-plan-context* cluster, not generic marketing strategy. The title tag is bleeding the page's relevance signal.
3. **Zero backlinks competing against DR 83-96 government, finance, and SaaS sites.** SERP top 10 = SBA (DR 91, 5,190 refdomains), Salesforce (DR 92), Adobe (DR 96), Investopedia (DR 92), Wolters Kluwer (DR 89), Australian gov (DR 90). Upmetrics has 0 live referring domains to this URL. No on-page optimization alone will move this page into the top 10 of the *exact head term* — but a structural rewrite focused on the higher-volume cluster keywords ("marketing business plan," "marketing plan for startup") is winnable.

### Traffic Opportunity (1-liner)

Exact head term "marketing strategy for business plan" = 10 vol / KD 51 / traffic potential 1,200. The real prize is the cluster: ~50+ related variants currently driving 2,000+ monthly impressions at 0 CTR. **Moving the cluster from avg position ~75 to top-20 would unlock estimated 200-400 monthly clicks** — primarily by retargeting the page from "head term theory" to "business plan execution" (the angle behind every cluster query).
