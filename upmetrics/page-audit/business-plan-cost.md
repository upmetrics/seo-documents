## 4. SERP & INTENT ANALYSIS

### What Google is rewarding

- **Dominant intent:** Informational with comparison/research overlay. Searchers want a price benchmark (a number), then a way to choose between options (writer vs. consultant vs. AI vs. DIY).
- **Content types ranking:** AI Overview at the top (with 8+ sitelinks from cited sources), 1 Reddit thread (#2), 5 cost/pricing-focused blog articles (positions 5-9), 2 startup-cost guides at positions 4 and 10 (intent mismatch — Google is hedging because the query is ambiguous between "how much to pay for the plan" and "how much my startup costs"). 1 sibling brand ([bizplanr.ai](https://bizplanr.ai/blog/business-plan-cost)) at position 7.
- **What this means:** Google strongly rewards (a) direct numeric pricing answered up front, (b) comparison structures (writer vs. consultant vs. AI), and (c) answers that an AI Overview can quote. The target page has the right structure but uses prose paragraphs where competitors use callout numbers, tables, and tiered packages.

- **Current page type:** Blog article / informational guide
- **Match status:** ✅ Match — but execution is sub-optimal
- **Why position 11 despite intent match:** The target page covers all three approaches but does not surface concrete numbers in the first 200 words, has no comparison table, no FAQ schema, and no "factors that affect cost" or "hidden costs" sections that 4 of 6 competitors include.
