## 2. Thesis & Key Messages

### Thesis Statement

> BrightPath After-School Academy LLC is a financially viable youth enrichment business addressing a documented care gap in Cedarville, Ohio — supported by strong local demand from 38,000 residents, a diversified enrollment model, and conservative financial projections that reach profitability by Year 3 and comfortably support the $120,000 SBA 7(a) loan repayment.

### Key Messages

1. The U.S. after-school care market exceeds $23B annually and continues growing as dual-income households become the norm — Cedarville has fewer than 3 licensed after-school providers for a community of 38,000
2. BrightPath's blended enrollment model (full-time, part-time, drop-in, summer camp) creates multiple revenue streams that reduce seasonal volatility
3. Marcus Thompson's 8 years of school administration experience and CDA credential position BrightPath with credibility that new operators typically lack
4. Year 1 projects $244,800 in revenue with a realistic ramp to 60 average enrolled children — reaching net profitability of $26,631 by Year 3 as enrollment scales to 86 average children
5. The $160,000 startup capital ($120K loan + $40K equity) covers facility buildout, a transport van, STEM/arts equipment, and 5+ months of working capital — a conservative cushion for the enrollment ramp period

### Writing Tone

> **For Sample Business Plan:**
> - Write like a professional business consultant drafting a formal plan for a bank loan officer
> - Third-person throughout — "BrightPath After-School Academy will..." not "We will..." or "I will..."
> - Every claim must be backed by a specific number, date, or named detail from the Business Profile in Section 1B
> - Financial sections must include the actual pre-calculated tables from Section 6B — do NOT invent new numbers
> - Replace vague language with specifics: "20% year-over-year enrollment growth" not "significant growth"
> - Use childcare and education industry terminology: "enrollment," "child-to-staff ratio," "licensed capacity," "enrichment programming," "parent engagement"
> - The plan should read as if it could be submitted to Cedarville Community Bank for the SBA 7(a) loan
> - Maintain consistent use of "BrightPath After-School Academy," "Marcus Thompson," and "Cedarville, Ohio" throughout

> **General rules:**
> - Target reading level: Grade 8-9 (Flesch-Kincaid)
> - Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
> - NO filler sentences, NO passive voice, NO AI buzzwords
> - See BANNED AI TERMS list in the Appendix

- 🟡 **SHOULD HAVE** — Important for depth. Cutting this weakens the plan but doesn't break it.
- 🟢 **NICE TO HAVE** — Adds differentiation. Writer can trim if word count runs over.
