## P0 BLOCKERS

**WRONG** (factual/terminology errors — fix first):

1. **Operating Income labeled as EBITDA** — "Operating income (EBITDA)" H3 — These are equal in this article only because depreciation sits BELOW OpEx; in a standard QuickBooks/Xero P&L (where the audience pulls from), depreciation lives IN OpEx, so Operating Income = EBIT, not EBITDA. Add a clarifying sentence so readers don't misread their own P&L.
2. **"Operating margin (sometimes called the EBITDA margin)"** — The 3 margins H3 — Same root issue: these are different ratios in standard accounting. Drop the parenthetical or clarify.
3. **"Profit you keep" without taxes** — Net income walkthrough — $60,000 isn't take-home for a sole prop/LLC owner; income tax + self-employment tax still come out at the owner level. Either add a tax line to the example or rephrase.
4. **EBIT definition omits amortization** — "Depreciation, interest, EBIT, net income" — EBIT = EBITDA minus depreciation AND amortization. Sweet Crust has none, so the math is fine, but the definition is incomplete.
5. **Industry margin tables don't reconcile** — COGS table vs. margins table — SaaS COGS 15-30% implies gross margin 70-85%, but margins table says 70-90%. Same problem for retail. Pick consistent ranges.
6. **Image5 says "Depreciation (oven, mixer)"** — full-year P&L image — Text only describes the oven ($40k / 5 yrs = $8k). Either the mixer is also being depreciated (text needs updating) or the image label is wrong.

**MISSING** (brief-specified content not delivered — fill second):

1. **Internal link: "direct costs"** → /blog/what-is-direct-cost — required by brief, anchor "direct costs" in COGS H3.
2. **Internal link: "money owed but not yet collected"** → /blog/accounts-receivable — required by brief, in Revenue H3.
3. **Internal link: "paying yourself a salary"** → /blog/when-to-pay-yourself-from-your-business — required by brief, in OpEx H3.
4. **Internal link: "what lenders look at"** → /blog/what-lenders-look-for-in-business-plan — required by brief, in Operating Income H3.
5. **External authority links** — HBS (vertical/horizontal analysis), Investopedia (single-step / multi-step), all required by brief but absent from the article body. SEC 10-K and IRS Pub 538 are nice-to-haves the brief also specifies.
