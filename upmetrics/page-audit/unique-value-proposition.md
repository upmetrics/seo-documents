### Top 3 Problems

1. **Angle mismatch with search intent.** The page is framed around "value proposition *in a business plan*" (investor-pitch lens), but the keyword "unique value proposition" has general marketing/CRO intent — every top-10 SERP result (CXL, Semrush, HBS, ActiveCampaign) is a general marketing UVP guide, not a business-plan sub-section. Google buries the page at position 70-90 as a result.
2. **Thin, shallow content vs. the SERP.** At 1,487 words the page is roughly half the length of the two top organic guides (CXL 2,290; Semrush 2,922) and misses the depth signals Google rewards: UVP vs. USP distinctions, named frameworks (Steve Blank, Geoffrey Moore), UVP testing methods, and real homepage teardowns.
3. **Zero authority + zero clicks.** The page has **0 live backlinks / 0 referring domains** while SERP competitors have DR 71-99 and 7-1,389 referring domains. Across 73 queries and 2,000+ impressions in 6 months, the page earned **0 clicks**.

### Traffic Opportunity

> Primary keyword KD 46, volume 2,700/mo, plus a cluster of ~30 "uvp / value proposition examples" variants. The page already collects 2,000+ impressions/6mo while stuck at position 70-90 — proof Google sees topical relevance but not enough quality/authority to rank it. A full re-angle + depth rebuild + internal-link/authority push could realistically move the cluster from **0 clicks today** to **40-120 clicks/mo (top 10)** or **200-500+ clicks/mo (top 3)**. This is a rebuild, not a quick win — the page is not in striking distance.

## 4. SERP & Intent Analysis

### What Google is rewarding

- **Dominant intent:** Informational (with strong commercial undertone — readers are businesses/marketers building a UVP).
- **Content types ranking (top 10 organic):** General marketing/strategy UVP guides and glossary pages — CXL (listicle of 7 examples + how-to), Semrush (what-it-is + how-to guide), HBS Institute for Strategy (concept page), ActiveCampaign & INFUSE (glossary definitions), Wikipedia (USP encyclopedia), YouTube (how-to video).
- **What this means:** Google rewards comprehensive, example-rich, generally-applicable UVP education from authoritative marketing brands — **not** business-plan-specific framing.

### Intent match / mismatch

- **Current page type:** Blog guide, but scoped to "value proposition *in a business plan*" (investor/founder lens).
- **Match status:** ⚠️ **Partial mismatch.**
- **Why:** The keyword "unique value proposition" is a general marketing concept. Every ranking page treats it as a marketing/positioning topic applicable to any business; the Upmetrics page repeatedly frames it as a component of a business plan ("What is value proposition in a business plan?", "How to write a value proposition for a business plan?"). This narrows relevance and signals to Google that the page answers a different (narrower) question than the searcher asked. The page can keep a light business-planning hook (it's Upmetrics' edge) but the **primary framing must become the general "how to create a UVP" answer** to compete.
