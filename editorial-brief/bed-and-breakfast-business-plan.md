## 2. Thesis & Key Messages

### Thesis Statement

> Havenwood Inn LLC seeks a $350,000 SBA 7(a) loan to convert a historic Vermont farmhouse into a 6-room bed and breakfast targeting leisure travelers in the Green Mountains, projecting $309,600 Year 1 revenue at 55% occupancy and reaching profitability in Year 2.

### Key Messages

1. Vermont's Green Mountains region draws 13+ million visitors annually, yet Cedarville lacks a quality boutique lodging option — Havenwood Inn fills that gap
2. The owners combine 23 years of hospitality operations and property management experience, reducing execution risk for a first-year B&B
3. Conservative occupancy projections (55% Year 1, rising to 71% by Year 3) align with Vermont Tourism Board averages for independent lodging
4. The SBA loan funds both property acquisition and renovation, with a 10-year repayment term that the business can service from Year 1 cash flow
5. Ancillary revenue streams (event hosting, experience packages, guest pantry) reduce dependence on room revenue alone and increase average guest spending

## 8. Content Outline

### Writing Tone

- Write like a professional business consultant drafting a formal plan for a client submitting to a bank
- Third-person throughout: "Havenwood Inn will..." not "We will..." or "I will..."
- Every claim must be backed by a specific number, date, or named detail from the Fictional Business Profile
- Financial sections must include the actual pre-calculated tables from Section 6B — do NOT invent new numbers
- Replace vague language with specifics: "55% average occupancy" not "healthy occupancy levels"
- Use hospitality-specific terminology: ADR, RevPAR, occupancy rate, OTA, PMS, channel manager, rack rate, shoulder season
- The plan should read as if it could be printed, bound, and handed to a loan officer at Green Mountain National Bank
- Maintain consistent use of "Havenwood Inn," "Rachel Nguyen," "David Nguyen," and "Cedarville, Vermont" throughout

**General rules:**
- **Target reading level:** Grade 8–9 (Flesch-Kincaid)
- Short paragraphs (3–4 sentences max)
- Include real examples, numbers, and specifics throughout
- NO filler sentences, NO passive voice, NO AI buzzwords
- See BANNED AI TERMS list in the Appendix of this brief

- 🟡 **SHOULD HAVE** — Important for depth. Cutting this weakens the plan but doesn't break it.
