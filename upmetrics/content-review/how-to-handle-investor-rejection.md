The 30% rule contradicts itself — "How to extract signal" says a concern in ~30% of no's is "worth looking into" (and the pattern table calls 30%+ a business issue), but "When the no is about the pitch" says 3 of 10 (30%) "is not a strong signal yet." — Reconcile to one threshold; present 30%/50% as rough heuristics, not precise rules.

3. Alternative-funding section's content lives only inside image1.png — the six paths/conditions never appear in prose. — Add them as text so the section survives without the image.
