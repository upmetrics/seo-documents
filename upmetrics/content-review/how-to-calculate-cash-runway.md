## WHAT'S WORKING

- **"What counts as cash on hand" is the standout** — including the right items and explicitly excluding undrawn credit lines, unpaid invoices, and deposits is exactly what founders get wrong, and it beats CFI, WallStreetPrep, and JPMorgan.
- **Gross vs net burn is crystal clear** — "burn rate is the speed, runway is how much time that speed leaves you" plus the $80k/$30k illustration resolves the most common confusion.
- **Consistent, credible founder voice** — first-person, no jargon, no fluff; reads like a peer who has actually done this.
- **Correct math throughout** — every calculation and cross-referenced number checks out.
- **Well-scoped FAQ** — four genuinely tangential questions, exactly as the brief directed.
