## 2. Why We'll Win

- **30-minute operational angle that no competitor takes.** Every competitor stops at "how to write it." We frame the plan as a tool to actually *run* the business with weekly/quarterly review cadences and a refresh trigger checklist — the post-write half of the job nobody addresses.
- **Real filled-out example with actual numbers.** Competitors give generic templates or fictional scenarios with vague figures. We embed a fully-completed coffee shop one-page plan with real revenue targets, cost lines, and customer counts the reader can study and copy structure from.
- **"What NOT to write" section.** No competitor has this. Cramming everything onto one page is the #1 failure mode for one-page plans — we explicitly call out the 5 cramming traps and how to avoid them.
- **Comparison table covering 3 formats (one-page vs. lean canvas vs. business model canvas).** monday.com mentions these in passing; nobody compares them head-to-head with a "use this when…" decision guide.
- **Embedded Upmetrics template + AI generator + downloadable file in one place.** Competitors point readers to external assets (or no asset at all). We give the reader 3 routes to a finished plan inside the article.
