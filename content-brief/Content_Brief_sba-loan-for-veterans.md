##### H3: SBA 7(a) Loan
> Max $5 million. The most popular SBA loan. Variable rates. Terms up to 25 years. Best for established businesses needing working capital, equipment, or real estate.

##### H3: SBA Express Loan
> Max $500,000. Faster approval (36 hours for SBA response). Higher interest rates than standard 7(a). Best for veterans who need faster funding. 0% upfront guaranty fee for veterans under the Veterans Advantage program.

##### H3: SBA Microloan
> Max $50,000. Average ~$13,000. Issued through nonprofit intermediary lenders. Rates 6-9%. Best for startups and very small businesses. 28-30 day average closing time.

##### H3: Community Advantage Loan
> Max $350,000. Designed for underserved communities. Credit scores as low as 140 accepted. Best for startups in underserved areas.

##### H3: Military Reservist Economic Injury Disaster Loan (MREIDL)
> For businesses suffering economic injury when an owner/essential employee is called to active duty. Working capital to cover operating expenses that can't be met due to the call-up.

#### FAQs (for FAQ Schema)

> Sources: PAA results + competitor FAQ sections

**Q1:** What is the SBA 7(a) loan for veterans?
**A1:** Explain that the 7(a) is the SBA's flagship loan program — up to $5 million, terms up to 25 years, used for working capital, equipment, real estate. Veterans get reduced guaranty fees (50% off) through the Veterans Advantage program.
**Source:** PAA

**Q2:** What disqualifies you from an SBA loan?
**A2:** List: existing federal debt in default, criminal history (certain felonies), business not operating in the US, not meeting minimum credit requirements (typically 620+), unable to demonstrate ability to repay.
**Source:** PAA

**Q3:** Do veterans get lower SBA loan rates?
**A3:** SBA interest rates are set by lenders within SBA caps — veteran status doesn't directly lower the rate. However, veterans save money through reduced or eliminated guaranty fees (0% on Express, 50% off on 7(a)), which lowers the total cost of borrowing.
**Source:** Related to "veteran sba loan rates" keyword

**Q4:** What is the 70/40 rule for veterans?
**A4:** Explain the VA disability compensation rule (if applicable to business context) or clarify that this is a VA benefits rule, not an SBA loan rule. If the writer can confirm the 70/40 rule context, provide accurate answer. Otherwise, clarify the distinction between VA benefits and SBA lending.
**Source:** PAA

**Q5:** How long does it take to get an SBA loan as a veteran?
**A5:** Timeline varies by loan type: SBA Express — 36 hours for SBA response, ~20 days to funding. SBA 7(a) — typically 30-90 days from application to funding. Microloans — 28-30 days average.
**Source:** Competitor FAQ (sbaexpress.loans)

**Q6:** Can a military spouse apply for an SBA veteran loan?
**A6:** Yes. Spouses of veterans, active-duty military, and widowed spouses of service members who died in service or from service-connected disabilities are eligible for SBA Veterans Advantage fee waivers.
**Source:** Military spouse keyword (20 volume)
