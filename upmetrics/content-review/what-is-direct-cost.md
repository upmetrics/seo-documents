## P0 BLOCKERS

**WRONG** (factual/arithmetic errors — fix first):
1. Candle $6.66 per-unit figure is unauditable from prose — "Calculate direct costs for a product business" — Body sums to $185 materials + $140 labor = $325 ($6.50/candle), not $333/$6.66. The $8 shipping-labels line that closes the gap exists only in image5.png. Fix: add the $8 shipping-labels line to prose, OR drop it from image5 and update $6.66 → $6.50 across all three occurrences (also cascades to $1,332 → $1,300 March COGS, 66.7% → 67.5% margin, and the $3.34/$3,340 sensitivity figures).
2. "75% of the total" service-labor claim is unauditable from prose — "Calculate direct costs for a service business" — Body's web-design materials list sums to $214 + $1,170 labor = $1,384 (84.5% labor share). To get 75% you need $1,553, which only appears once you add the $120 hosting + $49 plugin lines from image2.png. Fix: extend the body's materials list to include those two items and state the $1,553 total in prose, OR trim image2 and rewrite the paragraph as "labor is about 85% of the $1,384 total."

**MISSING** (critical absent content — fill second):
1. Two required brief references not placed in the body — "Why direct costs matter for pricing and gross margin?" — The brief's link tables list `https://upmetrics.co/features/calculate-startup-costs` (anchor "startup cost breakdown tool") and `https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/plan-your-business/calculate-your-startup-costs` as required, but neither appears in the article body.
