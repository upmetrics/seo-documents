## 2. Why We'll Win

- **Real numbers on every step.** Startup cost tiers (home-based vs. commercial, with dollar ranges), a pricing formula with a worked "$X per person for 20 guests" example, and profit-margin benchmarks (7–15%). Cordon Bleu and EventPro give zero concrete costs — we anchor every step in dollars.
- **A genuine launch sequence tied to a usable plan asset.** Competitors funnel readers to their own SaaS (Homebase, The Food Corridor's kitchen marketplace). We hand the reader a free catering business plan template + AI generator they can actually use — no gated product.
- **Dedicated "from home / cottage food law" coverage.** Captures the 250-vol "how to start a catering business from home" sub-keyword. Most competitors ignore the state-by-state home-kitchen legality that real beginners get stuck on.
- **Food safety & certification depth** (food handler card, ServSafe, health-department permit) — the universal gap. Every scraped competitor is thin or silent here, yet it's a hard requirement to legally operate.
- **A concrete "get your first 3 clients" framework + a real FAQ** built from PAA and Ahrefs question keywords. Cordon Bleu, The Food Corridor, and EventPro have NO FAQ section at all.
