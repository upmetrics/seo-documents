## P0 BLOCKERS

**WRONG** (factual/arithmetic/structural errors — fix first):
1. Geoffrey Moore's formula is missing the "Unlike [competitor], our product [differentiation]" clause — the most strategically useful part of the template. — `How to write a UVP (step-by-step)` — Add the fifth clause and rewrite the bookkeeping example to actually fill the [category] slot.
2. Coca-Cola "100-year history" is wrong by ~40 years — Coca-Cola was created in 1886 (140 years in 2026), the company incorporated 1892. — `Unique value proposition vs. USP, mission statement & tagline` — Change to "more than a century" or "nearly 140 years."
3. Garbled / duplicated sentence in the "How to write a UVP" opener — two unfinished rephrases stitched together with a missing close-quote. — `How to write a UVP (step-by-step)` — Rewrite to one clean sentence.
4. Garbled sentence: "A business plan typically backs up the executive summary, company description, and marketing plan." — `Why a UVP is important > Better investor and lender understanding` — Almost certainly meant "The UVP typically shows up in…"; rewrite.
5. Wrong noun: "Who am I writing this book for?" — reader is writing a UVP, not a book. — `Understand your target audience` — Replace "book" with "UVP."
6. Garbled prepositional phrase: "...complaining about, comparing, and asking for on competitor reviews on Reddit, niche forums, and market research." — `Understand your target audience` — Rewrite for clarity.
7. Steve Blank's formula + example is duplicated in italic two paragraphs later — looks like a leftover draft pass. — `How to write a UVP (step-by-step)` — Delete the italic duplicate (formula restatement + "Example:" line).
8. HubSpot screenshot used is the **Free CRM** product page, but the body text argues HubSpot does NOT frame itself as "another CRM" but as "one connected customer platform." — `Examples of unique value propositions > HubSpot` — Swap to a screenshot of the main homepage or the Customer Platform page.
9. FedEx body text quotes UVP as "Fast, reliable shipping customers can trust" but the image headline says "Fast and Reliable Shipping Worldwide" — verbatim mismatch, and FedEx's actual current tagline is "Where Now Meets Next" (2021). — `Examples of unique value propositions > FedEx` — Pick one wording and align body + image.
10. Writer-instruction paragraph ("This is your working document. Headings are clean — edit the content directly...") is not for publication — it sits above the Introduction H2. — `Top of article (pre-Intro)` — Delete before publishing.
11. H2 "Examples of unique value propositions" inverts the must-include keyword. — `Examples of unique value propositions` — Change to "Unique value proposition examples."

**MISSING** (critical absent content — fill second):
1. "Common UVP mistakes" section (brief flagged as medium-priority gap, covered by ActiveCampaign). — `Between Examples and Where-to-use sections` — Add 4-6 mistakes (vague claims, copying competitors, fluffy adjectives, serving every audience, no named differentiator, never testing).

Orphan one-line paragraph ("Before sending your plan out, show it to somebody who doesn't know your company.") is dropped into the investor subsection with no setup and no follow-through. — `Why a UVP is important > Better investor and lender understanding` — Delete here or expand into a proper stranger-test paragraph (or fold into the testing section).
