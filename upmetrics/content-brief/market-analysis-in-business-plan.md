## 7. Featured Snippet Strategy

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Featured Snippet Present | No standalone snippet, but AI Overview at Pos 1 + PAA boxes at Pos 5 |
| Current Holder | [SBA.gov (Pos 2)](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/plan-your-business/market-research-competitive-analysis) is most likely AI Overview source |
| Snippet Format | Paragraph + bulleted list (typical for definitional + components queries) |

**To capture the snippet:**
- Place a concise 2-3 sentence paragraph directly under H2 "What is a market analysis in a business plan?"
- Format: First sentence defines it. Second sentence states its purpose. Third sentence lists 3-4 key components.
- Target question: "What is a market analysis in a business plan?"
- Example structure: *"A market analysis in a business plan is the section that proves there's a real, sized, reachable market for your product or service. It tells lenders and investors who your customers are, how many of them there are, who else is selling to them, and why you'll win a share. It typically covers four areas: industry overview, target market, competitive landscape, and market trends."*

**PAA (People Also Ask) Targets:**
- *What is included in a market analysis of a business plan?* → Answer format: bulleted list of 6-8 components (industry, target market, market size, trends, competitive landscape, barriers, regulations) — answer in H2 "What to include"
- *How do you write a market analysis for a business plan?* → Answer format: numbered list of the 7 steps — answer in H2 "How to write… step-by-step" intro
- *How long is a market analysis in a business plan?* → Answer format: paragraph with range (typically 3-5 pages / 1,500-2,500 words) — answer in FAQ section
- *What is an example of a market analysis in a business plan?* → Answer format: paragraph + image link — answer in H2 "Market analysis example…"

> **Note:** PAA questions covered in H2 sections (first three above) should NOT also appear in the FAQ. The FAQ holds only the questions NOT covered in the body.
