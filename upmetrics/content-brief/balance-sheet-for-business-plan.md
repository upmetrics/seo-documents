- **We own the business-plan-projected angle the whole SERP misses.** FreshBooks, Shopify, and Bench are generic "what is a balance sheet" explainers that assume you already have books. Only the #1 result (bplans/Timberry) is business-plan-specific — and it's a dated, thin post. We write the guide for someone building a *projected* balance sheet for a plan they haven't launched yet.
