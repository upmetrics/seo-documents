## What's Working Well

- **Financial tables are airtight.** All three statements (P&L, Cash Flow, Balance Sheet) balance correctly across all three years. Every calculation verified independently. This level of financial rigor is rare in sample plans and will build lender confidence.
- **Competitive advantages are concrete.** Each advantage (Elena's PCC network, Marcus's operational background, CEFR tracking, local relationships) ties to a specific competitive gap rather than making generic claims.
- **Three-stream revenue model is well-explained.** Group courses, private tutoring, and corporate training each have clear pricing, volume assumptions, and growth trajectories.
- **Operations section is genuinely useful.** The daily schedule, staffing plan, classroom capacity math, and minimum enrollment thresholds give a reader enough operational detail to replicate the model.
- **Market data is well-sourced.** IBISWorld, Mordor Intelligence, Migration Policy Institute, Oregon DOE, and ACTFL are all credible sources (though framing and attribution need fixes).

## REVISION PRIORITY MATRIX

### P0 — Blockers — Fix Before Publishing

**WRONG (factual errors, contradictions — fix these first):**
1. **Hartfield/Portland inconsistency** — Thesis Statement — The thesis says "Hartfield, Oregon" but the entire article uses "Portland, Oregon." Update thesis to match.
2. **Duplicate paragraph** — Market Analysis — "This pipeline continues into adulthood..." appears verbatim twice (P8 and P11). Remove the second occurrence.
3. **IBISWorld industry decline** — Market Analysis — Article implies the language instruction market is growing, but IBISWorld says it DECLINED at 2.7% CAGR. Acknowledge the decline, then pivot to the instructor-led growth segment.
4. **Census stat misattributed** — Executive Summary — "19.4% of Portland residents" is attributed to "U.S. Census data" but links to portland.gov. Cite the correct source and verify the exact percentage.
5. **PCC walking distance** — Business Overview — PCC's nearest campus is ~5 miles from SE Division Street. Not walkable. Revise the distance claim.
6. **Prepaid rent error** — Financial Plan — Startup costs table shows "Prepaid rent (1st month): $1,800" but the lease is $2,100/month. Fix to $2,100 and adjust totals.
7. **Marketing budget chart** — Marketing & Sales Strategy — image1.png shows Year 1 at ~$3,000 but income statement says $6,000. Update the chart.
8. **Empty anchor hyperlinks** — Business Overview, Financial Plan, Conclusion, FAQ — 4 invisible links with no text. Remove or assign anchor text.

**MISSING (critical absent content — fill these second):**
9. **No risk/challenges section** — Conclusion area — Add "Key Risks and Mitigation" covering enrollment ramp, corporate contract delays, and founder departure.
10. **2 missing internal links** — Executive Summary & Competitive Analysis — Add "education and training business plans" link in Exec Summary and "vocational school business plan" link in Competitive Analysis per the brief.
11. **No summary financial table in Exec Summary** — Executive Summary — Add a small Y1-Y3 revenue and net income table for loan officer quick reference.

**WEAK (content exists but too shallow — deepen these third):**
12. **Tutoring hours decline unexplained** — Executive Summary/Financial Plan — Hours drop 34% from Y1 to Y2 with no rationale. Explain the shift.
13. **Generic competitor analysis** — Competitive Analysis — Only competitor types listed, no specific local competitors. Research and name actual Portland competitors.
14. **Owner salary growth unjustified** — Financial Plan — Salaries jump from $48K to $72K to $100K with no documented rationale.
