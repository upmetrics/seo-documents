- **First-person voice is consistent and compelling throughout.** The piece reads like a practitioner who has actually lived this problem — not a generic "7 tips" article.
- **"Bad financial planning wearing a noble costume"** — the deconstruction of the zero-pay badge of honor in the opening section is the strongest passage in the piece. Specific, contrarian, and shareable.
- **The pause/resume table** is the most actionable element in the article. The parallel Pause If / Resume When structure is well-executed and designed for readers who will return to this piece during a cash crunch.
- **Structural logic is sound.** The progression (reframe salary as expense → signals → how much → pause/resume → salary vs. draw → conclusion) flows naturally.
- **FAQ answers are clear and non-redundant** (with the exception noted in P1 below).
