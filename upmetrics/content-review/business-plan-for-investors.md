1. **3-vs-5 contradiction in "Why do investors need a business plan?"** — paragraph 1 says investors verify *three* things; paragraph 2 says a strong plan must "prove five things, fast." Pick one frame.
2. **"4-W framework" H2 contradicts body and image2** — body adds a fifth W, image2.png is titled "5-W Framework." Update the H2.
3. **image2.png "Why them" row** — image describes a customer-positioning question; body and the canonical Sequoia framework treat it as an investor-fit question. Regenerate the image.
4. **image3.png placement wrong** — hero/banner-style image with the article headline placed at the very END after the FAQ. Move to top of article.
5. **Y Combinator citation links to Ink Narrates** — anchor says "Y Combinator advises..." but URL points to a third-party blog. Re-anchor to ycombinator.com/library/8d-how-to-build-a-great-series-a-pitch-and-deck.

1. **Common Mistakes / 8 mistakes section** — promised twice (intro + transition before Plan-vs-Proposal), never delivered. Highest-impact single fix in this review.
2. **Body listing of the 6 executive-summary elements** — image1.png is the only place they're named; body says "covers six elements" but never enumerates.
3. **Must-include keyword "business plan template for investors"** — not present anywhere in the article body.
4. **Target audience demographics in Opportunity** — brief flagged this as a GAP; section is generic.
5. **5 of 6 brief-mandated internal links** — only `/blog/exit-strategies-for-business` placed; missing: `/blog/business-plan-presentation`, `/blog/what-investors-want-from-your-business`, `/services/business-plan-writing`, `/template/investment-company-business-plan`, `/features/business-plan-builder` (article uses `/ai-business-plan-generator` instead).
6. **Stray "Ink Narrates" attribution** at end of "Why now?" section — looks like a research-paste artifact.
7. **Cannibalization decision (BRIEF BLOCKER)** — `/blog/impress-the-investor-with-business-plan` (130 impressions, position 65) still ranking. SEO team must decide on 301 redirect before publish.
8. **Brief Q "What are the most important sections of a business plan for investors?" not in FAQ.**

**WEAK** (too shallow — deepen third):
1. **Unit Economics** — the brief's UNIQUE EDGE. Currently scattered across Revenue model / Marketing & sales / Financials with no dedicated subsection, no calculations, no benchmarks. Add an "Unit economics" H3 under Financials covering CAC payback, LTV/CAC, gross margin by business type, burn multiple, and runway with one worked example.
2. **Opportunity section** — 60 words of definitional prose, voice drift to textbook tone, semantically overlaps with Industry analysis below it. Either expand with target-audience demographics + USP framing (closing two brief gaps at once), or merge into Industry analysis and drop the article from "12 sections" to "11."
