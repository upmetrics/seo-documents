## 2. Why We'll Win

- **Only guide that ties contingency planning into a full business plan.** Every competitor treats it as a standalone corporate exercise — we frame it as a section inside the business plan document SMBs already maintain, which is Upmetrics's lane.
- **Real SMB-scale financial numbers.** We give reserve-sizing math (3-6 months operating expenses), insurance cost ranges, and a financial contingency checklist. No competitor cites numbers.
- **Key-personnel / bus-factor scenario.** Quora users ask "what's the contingency when a resource leaves?" — zero competitors cover this. It becomes a dedicated H3 example.
- **Embedded 7-step framework with triggers.** We define the concrete metric that activates each response (e.g., "cash runway drops below 60 days") instead of vague "when disruption occurs" language.
- **FAQ section with 7 unique questions.** Three sourced from PAA, four from Asana/writer-reader gaps. Only Asana has a FAQ — and theirs overlaps their main content.
