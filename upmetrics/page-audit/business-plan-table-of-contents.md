### Top 3 Problems (priority order)

1. **The page gets impressions but zero clicks. 0 clicks across 1,800 impressions in 6 months.** Position 13.3 average for the primary keyword puts the page on page 2; even when fragments rank in the top 10, the **title tag fails to compete** in the SERP. Title is awkward ("How to Write Table of Contents in Business Plan?") and gets crowded out by SBA/.edu/Bank of America in the same SERP.

2. **The article is half the depth of every page-1 commercial competitor.** 792 words vs. competitor median 1,757. The article is missing entire topics that GSC shows users are searching for: TOC types (standard / grant / internal), formatting rules, where the TOC sits vs. the executive summary, and a marketing-plan-specific TOC (192 impressions on "marketing plan table of contents" and 86 on "sample marketing plan table of contents" — currently ranked p25 and p34 because the page barely addresses the variant).

3. **Rankings have decayed sharply over the last six months.** Every top query has slipped 10+ positions Nov-2025 → May-2026 (e.g., primary keyword p8 → p21.8). Without a content refresh, this trajectory continues. Google is rewarding deeper, more recent commercial competitors (Plangrowlab updated 2025-06; Growthink updated 2026-03).

### Traffic Opportunity (1-liner)
Lifting the page from p13.3 to p5–7 on its 5 highest-volume queries (1,800 monthly impressions → ~3-5% CTR on top half of page 1) would put **~60–90 monthly clicks within reach** without expanding keyword scope — pure on-page work.
