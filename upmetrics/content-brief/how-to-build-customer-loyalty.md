## 2. Why We'll Win

> Differentiators that move us past Forbes / Zendesk / HBR despite their DR advantage.

- **Small-business angle, not enterprise.** Forbes, HBR, Zendesk, and Emarsys all write for marketers at scale. We write for a 1-50 person business owner who needs loyalty tactics they can run themselves — no CDP, no loyalty platform required.
- **Plan-context tie-in.** No competitor connects loyalty to the business plan or marketing strategy section. We bridge "build loyalty" → "how to budget and document this in your marketing strategy" — a unique entry point that drives users to our core product.
- **Concrete dollar examples.** LivePlan and most SERP results give abstract advice ("offer rewards", "personalize"). We use real numbers — what a referral discount actually costs, what CAC vs retention math looks like for a $50/order business.
- **Owner-operator FAQ.** Our FAQ targets the questions a small business owner actually types post-read ("How much should I spend on a loyalty program?", "Can I build loyalty without software?") — competitors stop at the textbook 4 C's / 3 R's.
- **Original framing: 9 strategies grouped by stage.** Group strategies by what to do at first sale → repeat sale → advocate stage. Competitors list a flat 7-10 tactics — we organize by the customer's lifecycle so the reader knows what to do *first*.

**Original value-adds competitors can't match:**
