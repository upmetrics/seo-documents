7. **Four internal links from brief not implemented** — Business plans section — Missing: Business Plan Software, SBA Business Plan Guide, Best SBA Lenders, SBA Loan With Bad Credit.
8. **Two empty anchor hyperlinks** — "How to find" and Conclusion — Invisible hyperlinks need removal.

9. **Services section too shallow** — "What does an SBDC help with?" — Five services get 1 paragraph each. Brief says "go deeper." Add 1-2 sentences of specific detail to each.

11. **Content overlap between two sections** — "How to find" and "How does process work" — Both cover going to AmericasSBDC.org and the intake form. Merge or differentiate.
